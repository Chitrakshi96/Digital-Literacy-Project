# Social Media Responsibility Checklist for College Students

## ✅ Do's
- Think before you post; ensure your content is appropriate and respectful.
- Maintain privacy by limiting personal information shared online.
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Be respectful and professional in comments and messages.

## ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share sensitive personal details like address, phone number, or passwords.
- Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.
- Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying.
- Avoid oversharing your daily activities or location publicly.
- Do not trust or click on suspicious links or unknown sources.
