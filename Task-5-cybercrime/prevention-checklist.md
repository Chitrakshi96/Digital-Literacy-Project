# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
### For College Students in India

1. Use **strong and unique passwords** for all accounts; avoid using the same password everywhere.
2. Enable **two-factor authentication (2FA)** on email, social media, and banking apps.
3. **Never share OTPs, UPI PINs, or bank details** with anyone — even if they claim to be from your bank.
4. Always verify UPI payment requests carefully; remember that **receiving money does NOT require entering your PIN**.
5. Avoid clicking on **suspicious links** received via SMS, email, or social media.
6. Do **not install unknown apps** or remote access tools (like screen-sharing apps) when asked by strangers.
7. Use **secure and trusted Wi-Fi networks**; avoid logging into sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi.
8. **Regularly update** your phone and apps to protect against security vulnerabilities.
9. Be cautious while using **online marketplaces**; verify buyers/sellers before making transactions.
10. **Log out** from shared devices and avoid saving passwords on public computers.
